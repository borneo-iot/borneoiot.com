The iOS release of **Borneo Aqua Pro** is now officially live on the Apple App Store.

This is the official open-source mobile app for Borneo-IoT devices, built to make aquarium lighting and device control simple across platforms. With the App Store release, iPhone and iPad users can now install the app directly, connect to compatible devices, provision Wi-Fi with Bluetooth, manage scenes, and automate schedules from a native iOS experience.

## What You Can Do with the App

- Discover compatible devices on your local network.
- Provision Wi-Fi over Bluetooth BLE without touching router settings.
- Create and manage lighting schedules with smooth daily transitions.
- Organize devices into scenes for quick switching.
- Keep control local on your network, even without internet access.
