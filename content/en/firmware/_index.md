## Highly Customizable

Our firmware platform supports nearly the full line of Espressif wireless microcontrollers, letting you choose the most cost-effective MCU for your product needs and significantly reducing BOM cost.

All peripheral connections, drivers, device names, models, and more can be customized to meet customers' personalization and branding requirements.
