## App Features

---

![](/img/icons/material-symbols/200/rounded/devices.svg)
### Cross-Platform
Use the same app on Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, and Linux. One familiar experience across all your devices.

---

![](/img/icons/material-symbols/200/rounded/auto_fix.svg)
### Zero-Config Setup
Turn on Bluetooth on your phone, open the app, and follow a few simple steps to connect your device to Wi-Fi. The app can also find compatible devices on your local network automatically, so there is no need to touch your router settings.

---

![](/img/icons/material-symbols/200/rounded/timer.svg)
### Dynamic Scheduling
Create smooth lighting schedules that mimic sunrise and sunset. Set it once, and the app will automatically keep your tank on time every day.

---

![](/img/icons/material-symbols/200/rounded/auto_awesome_mosaic.svg)
### Scene Management
Group devices and create scenes for different locations. Switch between "Daylight", "Moonlight", or "Maintenance" modes with a single tap.

---

![](/img/icons/material-symbols/200/rounded/performance_max.svg)
### Decentralized Control
No internet? No problem. The app can work entirely on your local network, so your devices stay private and controllable even when your internet connection is down.

---

![](/img/icons/material-symbols/200/rounded/design_services.svg)
### Modern UI
With dark mode and multi-language support, the interface stays clean and easy to use while still giving you detailed control.

## Technical Information

* **Framework**: Built with Flutter for a fast, consistent experience across platforms.
* **Architecture**: Modular design that makes it easier to support new device types.
* **Connectivity**: Bluetooth BLE for provisioning, WiFi (mDNS/UDP) and MQTT for control.
* **Compatibility**:
    - Android 6.0+
    - iOS 12.0+
    - Windows 10/11
    - MacOS
    - Linux (GTK+3)
    - Web (WIP)
